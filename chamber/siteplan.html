Site Name

Mongolia Vietnam Chamber of Commerce (MVCC)

Site Purpose

Based in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia, this website serves as the primary bilateral gateway to promote trade, investment, and economic cooperation between Mongolia and Vietnam. It provides a formal registration hub for active businesses, cross-border legal resources, trade data, and local networking events to foster strong international commercial relations.

Target Market

Importers, exporters, manufacturing entities, agricultural logistics coordinators, and corporate executives operating out of Ulaanbaatar, Hanoi, and Ho Chi Minh City looking to expand cross-border business.

User Personas

1. Mongolian Logistics Manager (Altangerel, 42)

Scenario: Needs to locate verified Vietnamese textile and agricultural suppliers. He visits the website to access the member directory and confirm trade compliance standards.

2. Vietnamese Agricultural Exporter (Nguyen, 35)

Scenario: Wants to register her business with the Chamber in Ulaanbaatar to secure local distribution networks. She uses the site to find membership fees and submit her online application.

3. Trade Attache / Investor (Batu, 50)

Scenario: Needs high-level updates on customs tariffs and bilateral agreements between the two nations. He checks the blog, news feeds, and calendar for upcoming trade summits.

SEO Plan

  • Keywords: Mongolia Vietnam trade, Ulaanbaatar business directory, MVCC registration, bilateral commerce, Vietnam import export.
  • Verification: Register the physical Ulaanbaatar office on Google Business Profile.
  • Backlinks: Establish mandatory reciprocal profile linking on all registered member company websites.
  • Analytics: Integrate native Google Analytics scripts globally across all active pages.
